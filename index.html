About NumSmu

NumSmu is a passionate engineering community with members from IIT(ISM) Dhanbad and industry professionals. We specialize in simulation and design services in fields like petrochemicals, aerospace, defense, oil & gas, and manufacturing. Our aim is to bridge academia and industry with hands-on engineering problem-solving.
