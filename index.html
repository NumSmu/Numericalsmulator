About Us

NumSmu is a vibrant technical community of IIT (ISM) Dhanbad students and professionals focused on solving real-world problems in petrochemicals, aerospace, defense, and more. We specialize in CAD, FEA, CFD, optimization, and scientific computing using SolidWorks, Ansys, Python, Julia, and more.

Courses We Offer

  • CAD Design (SolidWorks, Assembly, Sheet Metal)
  • Scientific Computing with Python & Julia
  • Machine Learning for Simulations
  • FEA & CFD using Ansys
